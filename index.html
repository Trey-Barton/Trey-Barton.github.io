About Me

I'm a Media Studies & Engineering student at Dartmouth College with a focus on human-centered design. I work at the intersection of creative storytelling, emerging technology, and digital media — from building AI-powered tools to producing video campaigns for nonprofits.

I've studied film and media industries in Los Angeles, luxury brand strategy in Milan, and have built projects with collaborators at Stanford and MIT. When I'm not designing or coding, you'll find me working on ceramics, exploring digital art, or figuring out the next creative experiment.
